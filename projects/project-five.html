Reconstruction of an Industrial Building into a Residental Block

Celje, Slovenia / 2024

The project explores the transformation of an existing urban structure through the introduction of gallery spaces and green communal terraces.

By integrating vegetation, shared outdoor areas and new residential units, the intervention creates a healthier and more socially connected living environment for young residents.

CONCEPT

Architecture Between Community and Nature

The galleries become spatial connectors that encourage interaction, improve circulation and establish a continuous relationship between housing, greenery and shared public life.
