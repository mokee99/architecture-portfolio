Cafe Interior in New Belgrade

Belgrade, Serbia / 2023

The project represents a contemporary café designed as a vibrant social space that encourages interaction, gathering and everyday community life.

Through the use of warm materials, soft lighting and an open spatial organization, the interior creates a welcoming atmosphere that connects people through comfort, conversation and shared experience.

CONCEPT

Architecture of Interaction

The interior is organized around fluid movement, visual openness and carefully framed spatial moments that enhance the social character of the café environment.

DRAWINGS Interior Elevation Spatial Organization
ATMOSPHERE

Soft lighting, warm materials and natural textures create an intimate atmosphere that encourages gathering, interaction and everyday social experience.
