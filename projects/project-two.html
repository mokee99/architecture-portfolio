Revitalization of the Bresternica Kayak Center Area

Maribor, Slovenia / 2024

The project reimagines the kayaking center in Bresternica as a place where architecture connects with nature and the community. The design follows the landscape, creating a seamless experience between land and water.

CONCEPT

Architecture Growing from the Landscape

The buildings follow the natural contours of the terrain and gradually emerge from the ground, maintaining harmony with the environment.
