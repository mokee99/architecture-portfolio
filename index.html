ABOUT

I am an architecture master’s student interested in the relationship between architecture, landscape and everyday life.

My work explores atmosphere, materiality and the emotional experience of space.

I approach each project through careful observation of context, movement and human needs.

I aim to create thoughtful, functional spaces that feel grounded in their surroundings and meaningful to the people who use them.

Feel free to reach out for collaborations, projects or conversations about architecture and space.
